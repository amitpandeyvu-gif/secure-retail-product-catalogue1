About Our Project

Secure Retail Product Catalogue is a frontend website created for Pop Culture Clothing. The project helps customers view clothing products and gives the business a stronger online presence.

Problem Statement

The client currently manages products manually, which can cause errors and make product updates slow. Customers also need a simple way to browse product information online.
