Project Description

The Secure Retail Product Catalog is a web-based application developed as part of the IT Capstone A project. The platform allows customers to browse and explore a curated catalogue of pop culture merchandise including items from Marvel, DC Comics, Star Wars, Pokemon, Harry Potter, and Anime. The system is designed to be secure, responsive, and user-friendly for both customers and administrators.

Problem Statement

Many pop culture fans struggle to find a single, reliable platform that brings together merchandise from multiple franchises in one place. Existing platforms are often cluttered, difficult to navigate, or lack security features. This project aims to solve that problem by providing a clean, secure, and easy-to-use product catalogue that allows customers to find and explore their favourite pop culture products quickly and safely.
