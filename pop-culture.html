About Pop Culture

Project Description

Pop Culture is a modern clothing storefront built for fans who want their favourite genres — sci-fi, superheroes, retro gaming, fantasy and anime — woven into everyday streetwear, not just novelty prints. The catalogue, shopping cart and admin dashboard on this site were built as part of an IT Capstone project, upgraded into a fully responsive, single-page experience.

Problem Statement

Fans of pop culture merchandise often bounce between cluttered, hard-to-navigate stores to find clothing that feels current rather than costume-y. Pop Culture solves this with one clean, secure, mobile-friendly catalogue — plus tools for a small team to manage stock and pricing without touching code.
